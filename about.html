Our Story

Built for South African Small Business

Local Web SA was started in Pretoria with one belief: every South African business deserves a website that's as professional as their work, without paying agency prices. We watched too many spaza shops, salons, mechanics and tradespeople either skip having a site entirely, or pay thousands for something that never delivered.

So we built the studio we wished existed: simple packages, honest prices in rands, a WhatsApp you can actually reach, and a site live in days, not months. Every day without a website is a day a potential customer chose your competitor instead. Let's fix that.

No team, no overhead, no account managers. Just one builder, your repo, and a clear shipping date, backed by hosting and care plans you can cancel any time. We keep everything straightforward so you can focus on running your business.
