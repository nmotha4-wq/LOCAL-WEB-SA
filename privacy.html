Privacy Policy

POPIA-compliant · Last updated 17 May 2026

We're a small studio. We collect the minimum information needed to do the work and answer your questions. No selling, no third-party trackers, no surprises.

1. What we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, WhatsApp number, email, business name).
  • Anonymised analytics (page views, country, referrer) via a privacy-friendly tool.
  • Project files you share with us during a build.

2. Why we collect it

To answer your enquiry, deliver your project, and improve this website. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it.

3. How we store it

Contact details live in our CRM (encrypted at rest). Project files live in a private GitHub repo we share with you. Analytics are aggregated and never tied to your name.

4. Your POPIA rights

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act, you can request access, correction, or deletion of any data we hold on you. Email info@localwebsa.org and we'll action within 7 days.

5. Cookies

This site uses no third-party cookies. The only cookie is a session cookie to remember your colour-scheme preference.
